Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 2 T butter
  • 8 oz mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 lg onion (diced)
  • 1 t paprika
  • 1 T ranch dressing seasoning (opt)
  • 1 T dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 c flour
  • 3 c beef broth
  • 2 t worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 c sour cream
  • 1 bag (12 oz) egg noodles

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook beef until cooked through. Remove from pan and discard grease.
  2. In large pot, boil water and prepare noodles to package directions, cooking slightly less than al dente.
  3. In same pan over medium-high heat, melt butter.
  4. Sautee mushrooms, garlic, and onion to desired doneness.
  5. Sprinkle flour over sauteed vegetables and stir, cooking, ~1 minute.
  6. Turn heat to low and slowly whisk in broth, stirring out any clumps before adding more.
  7. Simmer until sauce reaches desired thickness.
  8. Once thickened, stir in remaining ingredients (except noodles).
  9. Simmer about 5 minutes.
  10. Serve over noodles (or mix the noodles in!)
  11. Enjoy!
